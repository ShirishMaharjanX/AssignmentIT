About Asian Landscape Art

Asian landscape painting, particularly from China, Japan and Korea, has a rich history spanning thousands of years. These works often depict mountains, water, trees, and small human figures, emphasizing the harmony between humans and nature.

The delicate balance of ink washes creates depth through atmospheric perspective, while carefully placed elements guide the viewer's eye through a journey of contemplation and peace.
